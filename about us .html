Our Journey

Health Station Pharmacy Ltd & Mart was founded in 2025 , a passionate man with a vision to create a comprehensive healthcare hub that serves all community needs under one roof.

Starting as a small neighborhood pharmacy, we've grown into a full-service healthcare destination that combines pharmaceutical expertise with daily essential products. Our expansion to include a mart section in 2015 was driven by our commitment to provide complete convenience to our valued customers.

Today, we stand as a trusted healthcare partner for thousands of families, providing not just medications but also health screenings, wellness advice, and quality daily essentials. Our team of licensed pharmacists, healthcare professionals, and customer service staff work together to ensure every visit meets your healthcare needs.
